Savings & contributions

All contributions in SKYLERS INVESTMENTS HOLDINGS by ordinary members as a discipline through this program helps in getting entrepreneurs in the habit of borrowing and repaying for business growth, school fees, future family needs and other purposes including emergencies.

Business owners are required to contribute a minimum of KSHS. 120 weekly throughout his/her ordinary membership tenure. Non-business owners will be assessed by the loan officer and the SKYLERS INVESTMENTS HOLDINGS executive committee to determine reasonable contributions rate per week.
