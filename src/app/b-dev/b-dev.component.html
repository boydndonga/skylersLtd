Savings & contributions

All contributions in SKYLERS INVESTMENTS HOLDINGS by ordinary members as a discipline through this program helps in getting entrepreneurs in the habit of borrowing and repaying for business growth, school fees, future family needs and other purposes including emergencies.

Business owners are required to contribute a minimum of KSHS. 120 weekly throughout his/her ordinary membership tenure. Non-business owners will be assessed by the loan officer and the SKYLERS INVESTMENTS HOLDINGS executive committee to determine reasonable contributions rate per week.

Loans

Business Startup Loans

Purpose:

A loan to start a new business for a client without an existing business but has the desire to develop their business skills to support themselves and/or their families.

Terms:
  • The clients must have finished the BDS basic training and present a reasonable business plan and budget to qualify for a startup loan.
  • The client must have shown discipline of contributions and have demonstrative personal initiative to the loan officer.
  • Due to higher risk of these loans requires the approval of the BDS department head.
  • All the other SKYLERS INVESTMENTS HOLDINGS business loan requirements apply
Benefits:
  • rovides a client with the needed capital to start a business and grow an income as a means to support themselves and their families.
  • Allows ordinary members of the community to take initiative, empower themselves to become self reliant.
Loan Duration:

First loan - 9 months - 1.5% monthly on the reducing balance Subsequent loans will fall under the SKYLERS INVESTMENTS HOLDINGS Business development loan program.

Loan levels:

1st loan - up to KES. 30,000
